What we Offer

This exhilarating 1 hour voyage will take you out of the Camel Estuary and around the headland, travelling up the coast to the historic fishing village of Port Isaac. Experience the granite cliffs, rocky coves, headlands, and more all at high speed it’s amazing how a different perspective really intensifies the beauty of our wonderful cornish coast. You will get a unique view from the the sea of the Cornish fishing village where the ITV programme ‘Doc Martin‘ is set and filmed. An exciting high speed trip taking in the sights of our beautiful coastline.
